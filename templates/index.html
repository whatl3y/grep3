Each service is independent, containerized, and accessible without accounts. Pay per use with crypto via x402 payment rails.

Token price API supporting symbols and blockchain addresses. Query prices for Solana, EVM chains, and more via multiple providers.

git.grep3.com

Decentralized git hosting powered by Ethereum addresses. Push and pull repos using your wallet as authentication. No accounts needed.

Philosophy

Permissionless. x402 Powered.

grep3 services authenticate via Web3 wallets and monetize through x402 payment rails. No emails. No passwords. No vendor lock-in.

1

Connect Wallet

Authenticate with your Ethereum wallet. Your address is your identity—no accounts to create or manage.

2

Pay Per Use

Services are gated with x402 micropayments. Pay only for what you use, directly on-chain.

3

Own Your Data

No centralized databases storing your information. Outputs go to your storage, keys stay with you.
